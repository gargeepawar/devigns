Privacy Policy - Devigns

Last updated: 06 April 2025

At Devigns, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email, phone number, and business details (for project inquiries).
  • Payment details (processed securely via third-party payment gateways).
  • Website data, preferences, and feedback for service improvement.
  • Analytics data (via cookies and tracking tools like Google Analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Communicate with you regarding your project.
  • Process payments and send invoices.
  • Improve our services based on analytics and feedback.
  • Showcase completed projects in our portfolio (unless requested otherwise in writing).

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and tracking technologies to:

  • Analyze website traffic using Google Analytics.
  • Improve website functionality and user experience.
  • Run targeted advertisements (if applicable).

Managing Cookies: You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some website features may not work correctly.

4. Data Security

We take strong security measures to protect your personal information and ensure its confidentiality. While we follow industry best practices, no online system is completely immune to risks. In case of any security concerns, please contact us immediately.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data. However, we may share it with:

  • Payment processors (for transactions).
  • Third-party service providers (only when necessary for project execution).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

Devigns is not responsible for any financial loss, downtime, or third-party service failures after project completion. Clients are responsible for maintaining and updating their websites. Any future updates or additional services will be charged separately.

You can:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Ask us to correct or delete your data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
